Class Oath and the Class Mascot

The Oath of Class 9F

The class oath is a solemn promise taken by we students, and it holds significant importance in fostering a positive and supportive learning environment for our class. The wording of the Oath emphasizes the sense of unity and camaraderie among the students. By pledging to stand together, we students create a strong foundation for mutual respect and encouragement. Our oath also highlights the importance of knowledge and curiosity. This commitment to intellectual growth can inspire and motivate students to strive for excellence in our academic pursuits. Moreover, the oath addresses the challenges that students may face, such as difficult projects and demanding tests. By vowing to support one another, we students create a supportive atmosphere where they can rely on each other's help and encouragement.

Mr. Funky, The Class Mascot of 9F

Mr. Funky was not a student in our class; he was the guardian, the protector, and the caretaker of the Class 9F Oath. He was crowned the King of Class 9F and eventually became the beloved Class Mascot. Funky was held in high esteem by all his classmates and even by students from other classes. He was the embodiment of the oath's principles, guiding us towards unity and togetherness. Though his story remains a mystery, it's tragic that he is no longer with us. After living for more than 4 months and serving his duties on the Class Bulletin Board, Funky passed away in July 2024, leaving behind a legacy of leadership and inspiration. May he rest in eternal peace and may we one day avenge his death by finding the criminal that killed our beloved Funky.
